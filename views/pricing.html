Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards through our secure Stripe payment processor.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

What happens if I cancel?

You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. After that, your account will be paused and your data will be safely stored for 30 days if you want to return.
